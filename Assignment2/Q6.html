Delicious Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves of garlic, crushed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a pan and cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with the Parmesan cheese. Set aside.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, drain and reserve some pasta water.
  5. Add the hot pasta to the pan with pancetta and toss to combine.
  6. Remove from heat and slowly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding a bit of pasta water if needed to create a creamy sauce.
  7. Season with salt and pepper, and serve immediately.

Nutritional Information

This classic Spaghetti Carbonara recipe provides a delicious meal rich in protein, fiber, and essential vitamins. Each serving contains approximately:

  • Calories: 500 kcal
  • Protein: 25g
  • Carbs: 60g
  • Fat: 20g
